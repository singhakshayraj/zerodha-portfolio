How to get your enctoken

1
Open kite.zerodha.com and log in.
2
Open DevTools — press F12 or Cmd+Option+I on Mac.
3
Go to Application → Cookies → kite.zerodha.com.
4
Find the cookie named enctoken and copy its value.
5
Paste it above and click Save & Connect.
Note: The enctoken refreshes roughly once a week when your Kite session expires. You'll need to repeat these steps when that happens.
Token is stored in your browser only — never sent anywhere except Kite's API.
